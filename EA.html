Equality Act

The EA protects from discrimination against protected characteristics which are:

  • Age
  • Disability
  • Sex
  • Sexual orientation
  • Marriage
  • Gender reassignment
  • Pregnancy and maternity
  • Race
  • Religion and belief

Types of discrimination

There are two types of discrimination:

  • Direct discrimination-Treating someone worse due to a protected characteristic
  • Indirect discrimination-Creating a provision criterion or practice that excludes or prioritizes people with certain protected characteristics (with certain exempt scenarios)
