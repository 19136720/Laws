Regulation of Investigatory Powers Act 2000

The RIPA controls and limits electronic surveillance and investigation by specified public bodies like the police and government agencies allowing them to:

  • Demand internet service providers allow access to digital customers communications without their knowledge
  • Carry out mass surveillance of digital communications
  • Demand ISPs fit equipment to allow for digital surveillance
  • Demand someone hand over keys to encrypted information
  • Keep secret the existence of interception warrants and any data collected under them even from being revealed in court
