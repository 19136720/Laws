Copyright Design Patents Act

The CDPA protects creators and owners of of intellectual properties and original works from unauthorised copying adapting and distrubution

Copyright

The CDPA allows an individual that has created an original piece of work the right to control the use of their work. The Act includes digital works and the legislation covers:

  • Literary works
  • Dramatic works
  • Musical works
  • Typographical works
  • Sound recordings
  • Films
  • Computer programs

Patents

A patent allows an original idea within certain fields to be protected. The shape or configuration of a product or item is protected by design rights.

Trademarks

A trademark protects indications of the commercial source of a product or service like a logo and remain in force as long as they are actively used or registered.
