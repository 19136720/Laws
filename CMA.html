Computer Misuse Act

The CMA was created to protect and prosecute for computer related crimes that can include:

  • Hacking
  • Fraud
  • Blackail
  • Malware
  • theft
  • destruction

Offenses

  • Unnauthorised access to a computer
  • Unnauthorised access to a computer to facilitate further crime
  • Unauthorised access of a computer and modification of data eg deletion of data changing data
  • Making supplying or obtaining tools that can commit CMA offences
  • Unauthorised acts causing, or creating a risk of, serious damage

Convictions
